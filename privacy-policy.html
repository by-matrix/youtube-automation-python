Privacy Policy for YouTube Automation Bot

Last updated: September 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube Automation Bot ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service that automatically posts content to Pinterest and other social media platforms.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Pinterest account credentials and access tokens
  • YouTube video URLs and metadata
  • Social media account information for posting
  • Board preferences and posting settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Log data including IP addresses and timestamps
  • Usage statistics and performance metrics
  • Error logs for troubleshooting purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Automatically post your YouTube content to Pinterest boards
  • Generate optimized descriptions and captions
  • Provide analytics and performance tracking
  • Improve our service functionality
  • Troubleshoot technical issues

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Pinterest API to post content to your boards
  • With other social media platforms you've authorized
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS
  • Secure storage of API tokens and credentials
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information

  • Export your data in a portable format

Each service has its own privacy policy that governs your data when using their platforms.
